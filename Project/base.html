About Us

What is Legendary Motorsport?

Legendary Motorsports is a premier car dealership known for offering a wide selection of high-performance and luxury vehicles. Whether you’re seeking exotic supercars or top-tier sports models, Legendary Motorsports is the go-to destination for automotive enthusiasts.

Do you offer financing options for vehicle purchases?

Yes, we offer a variety of financing plans tailored to your needs. Our team can help you find the best rates and terms, whether through banks, credit unions, or in-house financing.

How can I schedule a test drive?

You can schedule a test drive easily by contacting us via phone, email, or through our website. Simply choose a convenient date and time, and we’ll have the vehicle ready for you.

What warranty comes with new and used cars?

Our new vehicles typically come with a manufacturer’s warranty, while our certified pre-owned vehicles include an extended warranty. Details may vary by model, so please ask for specific coverage information.

Do you accept trade-ins?

Yes, we accept trade-ins and offer competitive valuations. Bring your vehicle in for an appraisal, and we can apply the trade-in value toward your purchase.

What documents do I need to buy a car?

Typically, you’ll need a valid driver’s license, proof of insurance, and financing details if applicable. Additional documents like proof of income or residence may be required depending on the financing option you choose.
